De Bubbles Eatery – Campus African Delicacies

Site Plan Document

1. Site Name

Site Name: De Bubbles Eatery

This name was chosen to reflect the vibrant, youthful, and flavorful nature of the eatery. "De Bubbles" is catchy, informal, and student-friendly, aligning with the target audience—university students and staff. It also hints at freshness and joy, resonating with food culture.

2. Site Purpose

The site provides a digital space where students and staff can explore African meals offered at De Bubbles Eatery. It will showcase a dynamic menu, student meal packages, and offer essential info like operating hours, location, and subscription options for weekly/monthly meal plans.

3. Scenarios

  • Where can I find today’s special or the full menu of African delicacies?
  • How can I subscribe to an affordable weekly meal plan as a student?

4. Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #FF914D (Burnt Orange) – Used for headings, buttons, and highlights.
  • Secondary Color: #1F1F1F (Charcoal Black) – Used for background and body text for contrast.

5. Typography

  • Headings: 'Poppins', sans-serif – Clean, modern font for headlines.
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – Readable font for paragraphs and descriptions.
