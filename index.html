About Me

My name is Miracle Lawrence, a passionate educator and software development student with a strong background in mathematics and over six years of teaching experience. I’m deeply committed to helping students build confidence in learning, especially in math and science, and I create engaging educational content, including games and videos, to make learning fun for kids. I’ve completed the PathwayConnect program and am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Software Development, with a growing interest in tech-based furniture design. I also lead a programming study group and actively support peers in their learning journey. My goal is to combine creativity, technology, and education to make a meaningful impact.
